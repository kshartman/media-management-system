# ACME Brand Logo Placeholder

Place your company logo in this directory.

## Required Files

- **Logo**: PNG with transparent background, ~32px height (width flexible)
  Filename: `acme-logo.png` (or whatever matches your deploy.sh)
- **Favicon**: 32x32 PNG or ICO
  Filename: `acme-favicon.png` or `acme-favicon.ico`
- **OG Image** (optional): 1200x630 PNG for social media share cards
  Filename: `acme-og.png`

## During Deployment

The deploy.sh script copies these into the project's public/ directory:
  acme-logo.png    → public/logo-placeholder.png
  acme-favicon.png → public/favicon.ico
  acme-og.png      → public/acme-og.png

## Quick Placeholder with ImageMagick

  convert -size 200x40 xc:'#0066cc' \
    -font Helvetica-Bold -pointsize 28 -fill white -gravity center \
    -annotate +0+0 'ACME' assets/acme-logo.png
